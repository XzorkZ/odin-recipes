Papas con Chorizo

Some mexican food I foundd online!

Have you used chorizo before? You can usually spot it by the distinctive reddish tint that dried chiles give it.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Mexican chorizo
  • Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes in a medium saucepan until tender and cooked through
  2. Saute the onion in a large skillet until softened and translucent.
  3. Brown the chorizo with the onions until fully cooked through.
  4. Combine everything together and cook for 5 more minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper as necessary, and garnish with cilantro.
